My Skills

Graphic Design

As a communication designer, graphic design is my most powerful and efficient skill. This involves being comfortable and incorporating my visual creativity in the Adobe Creative Suite. Along with being able to put together stunning visuals, conveying emotion and storytelling are the two things that set my graphic design skills apart.

Branding

Branding is a strategic and creative process of creating and establishing a distinctive identity for a product, service, company, or individual to differentiate it from competitors in the minds of the target audience. It involves crafting a unique and consistent image that communicates the values, personality, and qualities associated with the brand.

Design Research

As designers, the medium of empathizing and effective inquiry is design research for us. To be able to solve a certain problem, not for our benefit, but by seeking user opinions, this skill becomes a crucial part of any design process.

Typography

Typography is like the fundamentals of graphic design. My experience when it comes to typography lies in calligraphy leading to digital calligraphy, typeface design, and expressive typography as an effective form of storytelling.

Digital Prototyping

Being able to design with the help of code sets us apart from other designers as we are able to prototype our designs ourselves to be able to convey them better rather than relying on external help. I have fluency in html, CSS, Bootstrap and Java scripts.

Social Media Handling

Effective social media handling requires a combination of creativity, strategic thinking, and a good understanding of the target audience. It aims to create a positive and engaging online presence while achieving specific goals, such as brand awareness, community building, lead generation, or sales.
